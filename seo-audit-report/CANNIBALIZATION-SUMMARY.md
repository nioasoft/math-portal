# SEO Cannibalization Audit - Executive Summary

## Overview
Analyzed the Math Portal (smart-worksheets.co.il) for keyword cannibalization across:
- 10 topic/worksheet generator pages
- 6 grade-level pages
- 12 help/education pages
- 18 blog posts

**Total Pages Analyzed:** 46+ unique pages

---

## Key Findings

### CRITICAL CONFLICTS IDENTIFIED: 3

1. **Fractions (שברים)**
   - 4 competing pages: `/fractions`, `/help/fractions`, `/blog/fractions-made-easy`, grade pages
   - Issue: Multiple pages targeting same keyword
   - Fix: Clarify hierarchy, differentiate angles

2. **Multiplication/Times Table (לוח הכפל)**
   - 5 competing pages: generator, help, 2 blog posts (anxiety + tips), grade pages
   - Issue: Blog posts compete for same search intent
   - Fix: Differentiate psychological vs. method angles

3. **Long Division (חילוק ארוך)**
   - 4 competing pages with near-identical content
   - Issue: Blog post duplicates help page
   - Fix: Consolidate or redirect

---

## MEDIUM CONFLICTS: 5

4. Word Problems - Multiple entry points
5. Geometry - Mixed page types (but better differentiated)
6. Decimals - Missing metadata creates ranking risk
7. Percentage - Missing metadata
8. Ratio - Missing metadata

---

### 1. Fix Metadata (HIGH PRIORITY - Week 1)
- [ ] Add meta descriptions to: fractions, decimals, percentage, ratio, worksheet/math pages
- [ ] Standardize and improve page titles (5-6 pages need work)

- [ ] Blog "multiplication hacks" - ensure it's NOT duplicate of help page
- [ ] Blog "fractions-made-easy" - verify unique parent-focused angle

### 3. Internal Linking (MEDIUM PRIORITY - Week 2-3)
- [ ] Link blog posts → help pages → generators
- [ ] Establish clear hierarchy for each topic

**Estimated Time:** 3-5 hours

| /geometry + help + blog | LOW-MEDIUM | Well differentiated |

---

## ROOT CAUSES

1. **Incomplete Metadata** (5-6 pages missing descriptions)
2. **No Clear Content Hierarchy** (unclear which page should rank for which keyword)
3. **Overlapping Blog + Help** (similar audience and intent not differentiated)
4. **Grade Pages Competing** (grade-specific keywords split between hub + topic pages)

---

## EXPECTED IMPACT

After fixing these issues:
- **15-25% improvement** in ranking clarity
- **10-15% increase** in organic traffic (consolidation)
- **Better user experience** (clear navigation, no duplicate content)
- **Improved crawl efficiency** (Google clearly understands page purpose)
